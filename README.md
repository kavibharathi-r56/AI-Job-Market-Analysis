# 🌍 Global AI Job Market and Salary Trends

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the global AI job market to identify trends in job demand, salary distribution, and key factors influencing compensation. The analysis combines Python-based exploratory data analysis (EDA) with an interactive Power BI dashboard to provide meaningful and actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify patterns and trends in the AI job market  
- Analyze salary distribution across roles, experience levels, and locations  
- Understand the impact of experience, company size, and employment type on salary  
- Explore in-demand skills required for AI jobs  
- Create interactive dashboards for better data visualization and decision-making  

---

## 📊 Dataset Information
- **Source:** Kaggle  
- **Domain:** Employment Analytics / AI Job Market  
- **Format:** CSV  
- **Records:** 15,000  
- **Key Features:**
  - Job Title  
  - Salary (USD)  
  - Experience Level  
  - Employment Type  
  - Company Location & Size  
  - Skills  
  - Industry  
  - Posting Date & Application Deadline  

---

## 🛠 Tools and Technologies Used
- **Python:** Pandas, NumPy  
- **Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn  
- **Dashboarding:** Power BI  
- **Environment:** Google Colab  

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow

### 🔹 Stage 1 – Problem Definition and Dataset Selection
- Defined problem statement and objectives  
- Imported dataset and explored structure  
- Used `head()`, `info()`, and `describe()` for initial understanding  
- Checked for missing values and duplicates  

---

### 🧹 Stage 2 – Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
- Verified absence of missing values and duplicates  
- Converted date columns (`posting_date`, `application_deadline`) to datetime format  
- Created a new feature: **Days to Apply**  
- Ensured all data types were suitable for analysis  

---

### 📊 Stage 3 – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- **Univariate Analysis:** Salary distribution, job roles, experience levels  
- **Bivariate Analysis:** Salary vs experience, company size vs salary  
- **Multivariate Analysis:** Combined impact of experience, employment type, and salary  
- Identified trends, patterns, and relationships between variables  

---

### 📈 Stage 4 – Dashboard and Insights (Power BI)
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard with:

- **KPI Cards:** Total Jobs, Average Salary, Maximum Salary, Remote Ratio  
- **Charts:**  
  - Salary vs Experience  
  - Job Trends Over Time  
  - Industry-wise Salary Analysis  
  - Company Size vs Salary  
  - Skills Demand  
  - Education Requirement Distribution  
- **Slicers:** Experience Level, Work Type, Salary Range, Posting Date  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Salary increases significantly with experience level  
- Roles like **Data Scientist** and **Machine Learning Engineer** are in high demand  
- Medium and large companies generally offer higher salaries  
- Full-time roles dominate and provide more stable compensation  
- Skills such as Python, SQL, and TensorFlow are highly valued  
- Remote, hybrid, and on-site jobs are almost equally distributed  
- Salary trends remain relatively stable despite fluctuations in job demand  
- Education level has less impact compared to experience and skills
